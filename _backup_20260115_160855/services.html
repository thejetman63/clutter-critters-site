FAQ

Do I have to be home?

Usually, yes — at least for the decluttering decisions. For some areas, we can work while you’re nearby.

Will you take donations?

We can sort and bag donations for you. If you want drop-off included, add it as an option.

Do you shop for bins/containers?

We can recommend products and create a shopping list. Shopping can be added if desired.

Is this confidential?

Absolutely. We’re judgment-free and discreet — your home is your business.

Not sure what you need?

Send a couple photos + a short note, and we’ll recommend the best session.
